Ai vs human text detector

In the age of rapid advancements in artificial intelligence, distinguishing between human-authored content and AI-generated text has become an important challenge. Our project addresses this issue by developing a robust detection system that can accurately differentiate between texts written by humans and those generated by AI models.

Using advanced natural language processing (NLP) techniques and machine learning algorithms, we analyze key linguistic features, sentence structures, and writing patterns that reveal the source of the text. This system is especially valuable for ensuring academic integrity, identifying machine-generated content in educational and professional settings, and maintaining the authenticity of written work in various domains.

Our solution is designed to support educators, researchers, and content creators in protecting intellectual property and upholding the quality of human-authored work, while promoting responsible use of AI technologies.
